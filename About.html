About Us

For over 35 years, PT Jakarana Tama has been a leader in Indonesia's food industry. Initially starting as a distributor, the company has evolved into one of the largest distribution networks across the country. In 1992, we embarked on a new journey, becoming a direct manufacturer specializing in processed food products.

Realizing the importance of business and product diversification, in 1998, we introduced the 'GAGA' brand, bringing a new identity to our portfolio. As we expanded our product offerings, we also broadened our distribution channels, ensuring that GAGA's unique flavors are available in every major city across Indonesia.
