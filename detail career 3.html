Company Profile

PT Jakarana Tama was established on June 20, 1980 based on Notarial Deed No. 107 of Kusmulayanto Ongko, SH as a regional distribution company in Medan, North Sumatra.

The main business of the company is to produce instant noodles, canned products, ready-to-eat sausages, and seasoning. Realizing the importance of business and product diversification, the company has invested in the 'GaGa' brand in our portfolio. The company also owns several other brands, such as 100, 1000, Mie Gepeng, Mie Telor A1, Otak-otak, and Sosis Loncat.

These brands cater to several different market segments and consumers. In line with the increasing number of product varieties, the company has expanded its distribution, scattered across all major cities in Indonesia.

Today, the company has equipped itself with international-standard product innovations, adequate and advanced production technology, and highly qualified experts to support the overall objectives of the company in pursuing its corporate mission.

The company owns one of Indonesia's most popular instant noodle brands, GaGa, which continually gains market share in an immensely competitive environment.
