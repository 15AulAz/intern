Production Manager (FMCG)

Ciawi, Bogor, West Java | Full Time

Production, Planning & Scheduling (Manufacturing, Transportation & Logistics)

Job Description

  1. In charge of evaluating the production process by controlling production parameters and raw materials in accordance with quality standards and production targets.
  2. Organize and supervise the implementation of the production process.
  3. Together with PPIC, arrange the production schedule and ensure that the product complies with the specified specifications and quality standards.
  4. Carry out Continuous Improvement in the production line to achieve effective and efficient production.
  5. Manage the team and evaluate performance periodically.
  6. Understand and implement the principles of quality and work safety.
  7. Ensure the implementation of the Quality System, GMP, Legal Requirements, and safety according to predetermined standards.
  8. Carry out production activities in accordance with applicable Standard Operational Procedures and Work Instructions.
  9. Create daily reports and dashboard reporting.

Requirements

  1. Minimum education: Bachelor degree in Engineering (Food/Mechanical/Electrical Technology) with GPA 3.00.
  2. Minimum of 5 years of work experience as a Production Manager in FMCG/Food factory (noodles and seasoning).
  3. Strong leadership skills to motivate and manage the production team.
  4. Good analytical, problem-solving, communication, and interpersonal skills.
  5. Able to operate Microsoft Office (Excel Intermediate) and SAP.
  6. High work motivation and attention to detail.
  7. Willing to be placed at the Ciawi Factory, Bogor.

Company Profile

PT Jakarana Tama was established on June 20, 1980 based on Notarial Deed No. 107 of Kusmulayanto Ongko, SH as a regional distribution company in Medan, North Sumatra.

The main business of the company is to produce instant noodles, canned products, ready-to-eat sausages, and seasoning. Realizing the importance of business and product diversification, the company has invested in the 'GaGa' brand in our portfolio. The company also owns several other brands, such as 100, 1000, Mie Gepeng, Mie Telor A1, Otak-otak, and Sosis Loncat.

These brands cater to several different market segments and consumers. In line with the increasing number of product varieties, the company has expanded its distribution across all major cities in Indonesia.

Today, the company has equipped itself with international-standard product innovations, adequate and advanced production technology, and highly qualified experts to support the overall objectives of the company in pursuing its corporate mission.

The company owns one of Indonesia's most popular instant noodle brands, GaGa, which continually gains market share in an immensely competitive environment.
